Week 3 - Loops, Arrays and Coding Style

Introduction

This week we look at loops, arrays and coding style. Three iteration constructs will be studied: while, do-while, and for loops. We will look at arrays as a mechanism to store large amounts of data of the same type. We will also study how coding style can facilitate the clarity of code.
