Your Midterm will be on the material covered up to and including week 5. It will have four parts (subject to slight changes):

Midterm Review

  • For a review we will look at a bank manager in charge of several investment accounts. Each account has cash, stocks, and a name. One can purchase stocks, sell stocks, add cash, and remove cash. The bank manager is a friend of the investment accounts so (s)he is capable of overriding the private members of these accounts. Several transactions will be performed to demonstrate the concepts learned in the first 5 weeks of the course. The code is:
    InvestmentAccount.h, InvestmentAccount.cpp, BankManager.h, BankManager.cpp, and BankMain.cpp.
