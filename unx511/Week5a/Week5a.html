Week 5a - August 2-6

Introduction

This week we are going to study message queues and semaphores. Semaphores provide us with a synchronization mechanism to protect shared resources used by multiple processes. We are also going to look at a comparison between all four IPC methods studied so far (sockets, pipes, fifos and message queues).
