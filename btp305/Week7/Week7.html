Week 7 - Midterm and Midterm Review

Introduction

This week will be your Midterm. Parts 1 and 2 will take place on Wednesday October 20 and parts 3 and 4 on Thursday October 21. The parts will be open all day for each day. Part 1 is multiple choice and is worth 15%. Part 2 is coding where you create a custom type and is worth 40%. Part 3 involves template functions and is worth 25%. And part 4 is debugging and is worth 20%.

Your Midterm can be found on BlackBoard under Test: Midterm. Sample questions can be seen under Test: Midterm Sample Questions. Comments on these sample questions can be found as follows:
